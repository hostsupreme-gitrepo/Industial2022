Our Program

Through our Shared Savings Program, we offer our customers the latest LED Lighting, HVAC, Solar, Power Storage and IoT Control technologies with NO MONEY UPFRONT OR FINANCED! Historically, the program has reduced our customers’ utility bills between 25-50%. “Shared Savings Program” 4D Services will conduct an assessment of the property and all utility equipment to determine if there is an opportunity for an equipment upgrade. The equipment upgrade yields two benefits, utility modernization and decreased energy usage; both resulting in a reduction in monthly utility bills. From the savings generated by the equipment upgrade, 4D Services profits by retaining half of the generated monthly savings. There is no upfront cost or financing required by our clients. We have an interest in each of our clients, we want to ensure that our clients’ utility bills are reduced as much as possible. In doing so, it is our responsibility to maintain the equipment to ensure it is running optimally by warrantying and servicing all equipment. For more information about the Shared Savings Program, please contact us at info@4D-services.com.
